Who am I?


I am a junior student at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, pursuing my Bachelor in Technology (BTech) degree with majors in Electrical Engineering with honors
I hail from Trivandrum (Kerala), the capital city of a state known for its variegated people, harmonious setting and, above all, high economic and social development.
On unofficial terms, I'm that kind of a guy who'd love to talk about almost anything in the universe (sometimes even outside the universe, if there's anything like that) for hours and bore others with long and sometimes funny stories.



Academic Interests

Alongside, I am also curious about programmable matter (matter which has the ability to change its physical properties in a programmable fashion, based upon user input or autonomous sensing) as well [Think of a rocket which can change its shape at each stage of the atmosphere and finally adopt the shape of a rover when it reaches Mars... ♥ ♥ ♥]
